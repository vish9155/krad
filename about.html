About- KRAD Global General Trading LLC

KRAD Global General Trading LLC â€“ Global Industrial Supply Partner.

KRAD Global General Trading LLC is a Dubai-based global sourcing and procurement company. As a global sourcing and procurement company, the firm assists contractors and projects with international market sources. The company operates offices in China, the USA, Italy, and India.

Our dedication lies in forming long-term partnerships with our clients by supporting their projects with sound procurement processes, compliance-driven coordination, and supply execution. Our goal is to assist our clients in simplifying sourcing through our global connections.

We are a procurement consultant organization, specializing in sourcing for engineering, industrial, and infrastructure projects, and we serve as a strategic partner to our clients by providing access to certified material, assisting with transportation logistics across borders, and aligning supply programs to specific technical requirements and the project timeline.

Who We Are

A Dubai-based global sourcing and procurement partner supporting contractors and industrial projects across international markets

Our Security

A compliance-focused sourcing approach with quality checks, documentation control, and supplier accountability.

Our Process

A transparent, structured process covering sourcing, coordination, and delivery to support smooth project execution.

Our Mission

Our goal is to deliver high-quality industrial, construction, and personal safety products, through reliable sourcing and seamless import-export solutions, as a globally trusted supplier. By delivering these goods and services, we empower our customers to build, operate, and grow their businesses confidently. We are dedicated to achieving excellence and integrity and establishing long-term relationships with our clients in the UAE and around the world.

Our Vision

Our goal at KRAD Global General Trading LLC is to become a leader in the supply chain for products related to engineering, industry, and construction on a global scale. We want to be recognized as the leader in providing the highest quality and the most innovative products in our industry while also providing excellent customer service. Together with our partners, we will be committed to developing sustainable practices to support innovative technologies to empower global businesses and communities while improving the well-being of the people we serve on every project that we take on.

Why choose Krad Global for your business

Krad Global General Trading LLC is an established and reputable global trade company that provides reliable supply solutions to customers for industrial, construction, and safety materials. We have a large global sourcing network and serve our customers with competitive prices, quality products, efficient logistics, and responsive customer service at all levels of the supply chain, including mission-critical industries.
