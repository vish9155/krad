Who We Are

Krad Global General Trading LLC, based in Dubai with International Offices in China, USA, Italy, and India, is a trusted Global Partner for Building, Construction, Industrial, Engineering, Oil & Gas, Water, and Infrastructure Material Supply. We combine Global Reach with Technical Expertise to deliver reliable, compliant, and cost-efficient solutions to Contracting Companies and Industrial Clients.

We operate through three focused Divisions, each designed to support the complex needs of Contractors, Industries, and Infrastructure Projects across the UAE and international markets.
