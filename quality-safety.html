Introduction-Industrial Safety Products Supplier in UAE

KRAD Global General Trading LLC is an established supplier of industrial safety equipment in the United Arab Emirates. We provide reliable and certified industrial safety products to construction companies, oil and gas companies, EPC contractors, and industrial facilities across the UAE, as well as to organizations throughout the world for all local, regional, and international projects.

As a long-term provider of quality safety equipment, we maintain a large stock of PPE (Personal Protective Equipment) and other site safety products for use in multiple locations within the UAE and internationally. The combination of our global sourcing capabilities, competitive pricing, and logistical understanding allows us to provide a consistent supply of compliant, competitively priced products while providing our partners with an opportunity to create a safe, compliant, and productive work environment.

Wide Range of Safety Products

Required protective and safety items include the following types of Personal Protective Equipment (PPE):

  • Head Protection (hard hat/safety helmet)
  • Hand Protection (glove or other)
  • Footwear Protection (safety shoe)
  • Flame-Resistant Workwear (Coverall, Shirt, or Pants)
  • Eye Protection (safety goggles)

Each product has been sourced globally from reliable manufacturers, thus providing you with ample stocks of everything you require at all times!!

Quality & Compliance

The safety products that are provided by KRAD Global General Trading LLC conform to required safety regulations and levels of performance. Our products have:

  • Compliance certification
  • Verification of quality by international suppliers
  • The ability to trace the source of the item as well as its documentation

We guarantee your personnel will have dependable and compliant safety products for work.

Stock & Supply Support

We are an exporter and stockist of safety products, so we can provide you with:

  • Immediate Stock for Urgent Site Requirements
  • Bulk And Schedule Supplies
  • Help With Export Shipment And Logistics
  • Competitive Pricing And Procurement Support

Applications & Use Cases

We're happy to offer our comprehensive safety solutions to protect employees who work in:

  • Construction Sites and Other Infrastructure Projects
  • Oil and Gas Refineries and Industrial Facilities
  • The Fabrication/Assembly Processes within an Industrial Workspace
  • Warehousing and Logistics Businesses

The safety products above will help protect the safety of employees while ensuring compliance with the applicable regulatory requirements related to employee safety and, in addition, will promote safe practices in the workplace.
