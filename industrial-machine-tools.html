KRAD Global General Trading LLC is one of the top suppliers of industrial tools and machines in the United Arab Emirates, where we provide our customers with the tools they need to perform their jobs effectively. Our focus is on providing construction companies, fabrication workshops, EPC contractors, and many other types of industrial operations with quality, reliable, and high-performance workshop tools and precision instruments, as well as assembly machines and other types of equipment that will allow them to succeed on the job.

We source all of our products from verified exporters around the globe, so we can ensure that every tool or machine we sell is high quality and will perform as required. As an established stockist and exporter of industrial tools and machines, we provide project workflows with competitive pricing, dependable inventory and a streamlined logistics operation to deliver and meet all local and international deliveries for your projects.

High-Quality Industrial Tools & Machines

We provide a broad range of industrial tools and equipment, which are designed for use in fabrication, installation, repair and building:

  • Hand Tools: Spanners, Hammers, Wrenches, Cutting Tools
  • Power Tools:Impact Wrenches, Drill Presses, Grinders, Torque Tools
  • Workshop Equipment:Pipe Benders, Magnetic Drilling Machines, Flange Joint Testing Machines
  • Measuring Instruments:Micrometres, Vernier Callipers, Measuring Devices
  • Heavy Equipment: Bolting Equipment, Boring Machines

All products are supplied by reliable exporters and are in stock, ready to ship immediately upon receipt of payment. All products have been tested for quality prior to shipping.

Applications & Industry Uses

All of our industrial tools and equipment are perfect for:

  • On-site construction and assembly
  • Mechanical fabrication and workshop work
  • Oil and gas field maintenance and installations
  • Infrastructural and plant industrial projects
  • Supply programmes for EPC project tools.

No matter if you are doing precision work or require heavy duty, our tools will help with the efficiency, accuracy, and safety of your work.

Quality Assurance & Supplier Standards

KRAD Global General Trading LLC possesses the following advantages:

  • Tools and machines are purchased through reliable export partners.
  • Compliance with international quality standards, as well as with performance criteria according to international standards
  • Quality control before sending out the goods.
  • Traceability and documentation for purchasing.

This strategy will provide the customer with confidence in the materials and equipment.

Stock & Supply Chain Support

We function as a dependable tool and machine supplier and exporter by providing:

  • Immediate availability of inventory for urgent requirements
  • Bulk and scheduled deliveries for project continuation
  • Logistics support: documentation, export coordination.
  • Competitive pricing, procurement support.

Our supply chain is set to service both local (in-country) projects within the UAE as well as deliver overseas projects efficiently and transparently.
