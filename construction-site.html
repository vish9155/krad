Construction Site Solutions & Materials – KRAD Global General Trading LLC

  • Home
  • Construction Site
KRAD Global General Trading LLC offers full construction site solutions and products for contractors and developers of infrastructure, EPCs, and industrial projects. We supply high-quality scaffolding, building supplies (tools, hardware) and safety equipment to assist with all phases of your construction project — allowing your job to be more secure and operate more productively while keeping to your timelines. As a global supplier and exporter, we can coordinate your material requirements, provide logistical assistance, and perform phased delivery of materials—all to facilitate the complex planning and execution of your construction project.

Scaffolding & Access Solutions

A trustworthy building site starts by having secure and adaptable entry systems in place:

  • Scaffolding Material & Components
  • Aluminum Scaffolding Systems are very lightweight and simple to assemble
  • Scaffolding Tube and Fittings—Great Design for Structural Support
  • Steel Scaffold Boards—Very Rugged Deck for Supporting Work Platforms

All of these scaffold items were made to be durable, safe, and in line with the business standards for scaffolding to help reduce site risk and improve productivity.

Site Tools, Machines & Hardware

Our company carries a wide selection of equipment and tools essential for performing construction activities. These include:

  • construction tools and machinery—all types of pneumatic or electric tools, machines, etc.
  • hardware and fasteners—bolts, nuts, screws, washers, eyebolts, and stud bolts
  • Shop support tools—torque wrenches, pipe benders, portable machines

By providing tools for installation, repair, and onsite fabrication, project teams have what they need to complete their work accurately and quickly.

Site Safety & Personal Protective Gear

At construction sites, compliance with safety regulations is critical. Employers rely on quality safety gear to help ensure employees remain safe while they work.

Some common items that contractors utilize include:

  • Protective hats and helmets
  • Protective footwear and gloves
  • Protective eyewear and goggles
  • Flame-resistant coveralls and workwea

Contractors make use of these types of safety solutions to help them enforce employee compliance, reduce the number of accidents, and keep equipment and personnel safe at every level of activity on a jobsite.

Project-Focused Site Material Support

KRAD Global General Trading LLC is your trusted partner for all of your construction site material needs. We provide you with the following:

  • Quick access to materials for urgent and bulk orders with guaranteed stock levels
  • Scheduled phased supply schedules based on the project milestones and schedules
  • Export logistics support for deliveries to your projects around the world
  • Material specification and project coordination support

KRAD assists in the timely delivery of materials to your construction project — whether it be in the initial phases of mobilizing or later phases — in order to minimize downtime in your project and optimize the efficiency of your project.
