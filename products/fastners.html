Industrial Bolts, Nuts & Fasteners for Construction and Oil & Gas Projects

Trusted Fasteners Supplier in UAE—KRAD Global General Trading LLC Fasteners Suppliers is dedicatedly providing reliable fastening solutions for construction, O&G, infrastructure, and industrial projects by providing reliable fastening solutions; Krad Global Fastener Supply provides a large variety of high-quality fastener products that have been developed to provide maximum performance in often extreme job-site conditions. As such, our fasteners are carefully selected based on their ability to provide optimum performance in terms of high strength, load stability, and long-term durability. Krad Global Fasteners Suppliers supplies a comprehensive range of industrial fasteners, including bolts, nuts, washers, threaded rods, and many other types of industrial fasteners for use in structural, mechanical, and fabrication applications. All products supplied by Krad Global are directly sourced from manufacturers based on client specifications and meet international standards for use and application.
