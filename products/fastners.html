Carbon Steel & Industrial Pipes for Oil and Gas Industry

KRAD Global General Trading LLC is a supplier of stainless steel piping solutions in the United Arab Emirates. Contractors and industrial customers can trust us to provide the industrial pipes they need for their demanding projects in the UAE. The pipes we offer have been carefully sourced to comply with the performance, durability, and compliance requirements of the United Arab Emirates oil and gas industry and for use in construction and infrastructure projects. Along with this, we have partnered with approved manufacturers to ensure that our customers receive stainless and carbon steel pipes that conform to international standards and their specific project requirements. As experienced pipe suppliers in the UAE, we know how important it is to provide our customers with a consistent quality of materials, the appropriate documentation, and good delivery times for their pipe projects. Our supply program provides a practical solution to assist with the timing of our customers' project completion and to ensure that they are able to receive the materials they need in order to achieve a successful project.
