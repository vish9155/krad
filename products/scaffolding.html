Construction and Industrial Scaffolding Systems for Project Environments

Scaffolding is an essential site element, impacting access and productivity, as well as worker safety. KRAD Global General Trading LLC has developed scaffolding products specifically intended to provide stable work platforms and reliable access opportunities for construction and maintenance projects. Each site and project will have its own unique load type, height, and worksite constraints; therefore, it is important to select the right scaffolding material and type to meet the requirements of each project's environment.

In addition to providing the contractor with the appropriate type of scaffold, KRAD also provides pipe scaffold system scaffold components and support, creating the opportunity for the contractor to create a practical, compliant scaffold system at any job site. All of the materials we supply for scaffolding are sourced with an emphasis on structural integrity, dimensional accuracy, and durability while being repeatedly used at the project site. KRAD's consistent supply of reliable material and project-ready scaffold products provides the construction site team members with confidence in their ability to maintain safety standards, work better, and perform their jobs without fear of falling or having an accident while performing their work at the site of their project.
