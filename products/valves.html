High-Performance Flow Control Valves for Industrial Use

KRAD Global General Trading LLC manufactures high-quality industrial flanges to provide reliable, leak-free connections for heavy-duty pipe installations. The majority of our products are used in oil and gas, construction, infrastructure, and industrial processing, where system integrity and long-term performance are critical. Each of our flange products is selected based on its strength, dimensional accuracy, and ability to perform well in demanding conditions. All Krad Global flanges are produced to meet international standards, which allows users to use the same type of flange with different pressure ratings, service environments, and materials. Krad Global will support and deliver our flange products for both standard piping installations and specific complex project designs with continuous production quality, dependable supply, and smooth installation. As part of our commitment to supporting the needs of all our customers, Krad Global will work closely with project management teams to ensure timely delivery, consistent product performance, and support throughout the life cycle of the piping system.
