High-Strength Steel Flanges for Oil, Gas & Infrastructure Applications

KRAD Global General Trading LLC manufactures and supplies a variety of industrial pipe flanges for high strength, high accuracy, and long-term durability in a wide range of extreme conditions. We manufacture flanged fittings that are designed to provide secure, leak-proof connections to piping systems while allowing for the desired level of pressure and temperature and designed to meet the highest level of industry safety standards. The oil and gas extraction industry, the construction industry, and many large infrastructure projects typically use industrial pipe flanges. When we manufacture all types of industrial pipe flanges, we adhere to very stringent tolerance and quality control requirements. We securely partner with manufacturers whom we can trust to produce all types of carbon steel, alloy steel, and stainless steel industrial pipe flanges, ensuring a high level of consistent quality and performance throughout. Krad Global provides businesses with durable products and precise fitting sizes so they can minimize costly disruptions in their operations and can focus on achieving their goals.
